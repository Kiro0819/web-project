About Us

Our Mission is to open up opportunities for all gamers where they could queue up games with other players, seeking out to improve themselves to reach a professional level player, and be able to give them a possibility to turn their enjoyment into carreers.


There are Coaches and Mentors to choose from with each of them specializing in different types of gaming genre that will help you achieve your success as a gamer.

Kiro specialize in fighting game genre. He teaches you how to create neverending combos that will help you beat your opponents.

Jadedbrookey specialize in both FPS games and MOBA games where she could help multiple gamers in order for them to achieve high levels in their ranks. She is also a streamer where she can help gamers who are interested in being a streamer.

Melvin specialize in MOBA games where he could teach hidden mechanics of every champions especially in League of Legends. He teaches players on how to conduct wave minion management and econ manage to ensure that players are ahead agaisnt their opponents.

Zere specialize in FPS where he could teach about crosshair alignments and strategic planning by utilities available in game. He is a streamer and also a pro player capable of teaching players to reach levels of professional players.
